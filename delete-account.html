Delete Your SnapSolve Account

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your homework images, solve history, and feedback will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the SnapSolve app:

  1. Open the SnapSolve app on your phone
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top-left corner of the dashboard
  3. In the Profile Details dialog, scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

That's it — your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and display name
  • All homework images you uploaded
  • All solve history and solution records
  • All feedback (ratings and comments)
  • Your subscription status
  • Your device identifier

What Data Is Retained

The following data is retained after account deletion as required by law:

  • Payment records: Retained for 8 years as required by Indian tax regulations
  • Free trial abuse flags: Retained to prevent system abuse (does not contain personal data)
